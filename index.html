Bio

Over the years I've run my game studio, created adtech & monetization products, worked as a freelance consultant & contractor among other things.

I have developed realtime 3D applications for over a dozen different industries ranging from military to arts & humanities. From video streaming R&D to Cyanide crisis training simulators.

Been developing using Unity since 2011. My open source work features popular Unity repositories for physics, AI, networking, audio/video, utilities.

In constant, practical pursuit of software craftsmanship.
