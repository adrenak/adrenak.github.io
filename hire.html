Code Quality
Having worked with a lot of Unity3D code over several years, I approach programming with good foresight. My methodology is to make modular programs. This means they can be reused across projects and come together to work reliably.

Readable, well documented, sufficiently automated and human error resistant code is worth a lot more than something that just works and I encourage my clients to treat software as among their most important assets and to make them reusable.
